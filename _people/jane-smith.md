Dr. Jane Smith is the Principal Investigator of the AgroSystems Lab. She received her PhD in Crop Science from Cornell University. Her research focuses on the intersection of agriculture, technology, and sustainability.

She has published over 50 peer-reviewed papers and is a frequent speaker at international conferences.
