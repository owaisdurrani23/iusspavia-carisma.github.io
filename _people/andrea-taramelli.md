Andrea Taramelli is a full professor at IUSS Pavia working on image processing and remote sensing for environmental monitoring, security, agriculture and civil protection. The IUSS contact page notes that he leads national, European, ASI and international projects and develops algorithms and methods for monitoring climate-sensitive environments.
